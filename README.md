## Project Overview:
For this project, We took one of our group memebers first capstone project for MYSQL and developed a Power BI report to provide the State Sales team with clear, actionable insights into business performance. Our analysis focused on identifying sales trends over time, highlighting recent sales performance, and uncovering key patterns that can support decision-making. As part of the report, we identified meaningful insights that could help territory and store managers better understand their markets. 
## Deliverables:
- A line chart showing sales trends over the full period covered in the data
- Matrix Chart of Store Sales within Florida 
- A bar chart showing sales performance by category
- A donut chart showing relative sales for Florida compared to the South region
- Marketing recomondations page
